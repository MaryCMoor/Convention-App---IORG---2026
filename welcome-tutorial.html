Event Schedule

View the complete convention schedule with all events, workshops, and activities.

  • 📅 View by Day: Filter events by Friday, Saturday, or Sunday
  • ⭐ Favorite Events: Tap the star icon to add events to your personal schedule
  • 🔍 Search: Quickly find events by name or speaker
  • ⚡ Required Events: Events marked in gold are required for your role
  • ℹ️ Event Details: Tap any event to see full description, location, and time
⭐

My Schedule

Your personalized schedule with only the events you've favorited plus any required events.

  • ⭐ Your Favorites: See all events you've starred in one place
  • 🎯 Required Events: Automatically includes events required for your role
  • 📥 Export: Download your schedule to share or print
  • 🗓️ Organized by Day: Easily see your daily agenda
  • 🔔 Stay on Track: Keep track of what you need to attend
👤

Your Profile

Manage your personal information and preferences.

  • 📋 Personal Info: Update your name, email, phone, and assembly
  • 🚨 Emergency Contact: Required - Add emergency contact information
  • 🍽️ Dietary Needs: Let us know about dietary restrictions and allergies
  • ♿ Accessibility: Request accommodations for hearing, vision, or mobility
  • 📝 Role Changes: Request role changes for admin approval
  • 💾 Auto-Save: All changes are saved to your device
